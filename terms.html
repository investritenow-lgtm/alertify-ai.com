02

Description of Service

Alertify.AI is an Android application that uses artificial intelligence to help detect potential scams in SMS messages, clipboard content, phone calls, on-screen text, and documents. The App includes five protection modules:

  • SMS Shield — analyzes incoming text messages for scam patterns
  • Clipboard Monitor — scans copied text for phishing links and social engineering attempts
  • Accessibility Monitor — detects scam content visible on your screen across all apps
  • Call Screening — identifies potentially fraudulent incoming phone numbers
  • Document Scanner — analyzes photos of physical documents for fraud indicators

The App is provided as a protective tool only. It does not guarantee detection of all scams or fraudulent activity.
