Privacy Policy

Effective January 1, 2025 · Last updated March 2025 · Version 1.0
Plain English Summary: Alertify.AI only collects what is needed to detect scams. We do not sell your data. Message content is analyzed and immediately discarded — never stored on our servers.

1

Information We Collect

Alertify.AI collects only what is needed to provide scam detection:

  • Clipboard content — text you copy is temporarily analyzed then immediately discarded
  • SMS messages — incoming texts are analyzed for threat detection
  • On-screen text — when Accessibility Monitor is enabled, visible text is analyzed for scam patterns
  • Phone numbers — incoming call numbers are analyzed when Call Screening is enabled
  • Push notification token — used only to deliver threat alerts to your device
  • Device identifier — basic identifier required for push notifications

We do not collect your name, email, location, photos, or any personal information beyond the above.

2

How We Use Information

Information collected is used exclusively for:

  • Analyzing content for scam, phishing, and social engineering threats in real time
  • Delivering push notifications when threats are detected
  • Improving scam detection accuracy

We do not use your data for advertising, profiling, or any purpose unrelated to scam detection.

3

Data Storage

Alert history is stored locally on your device. You can delete all alerts at any time from Settings → Data → Clear All Alerts.

Content sent to our analysis server is processed in real time and immediately discarded. We do not maintain logs of analyzed content.

4

Third-Party Services

  • Anthropic Claude API — AI analysis of potentially scam content
  • Google Safe Browsing API — URL threat checking. Only the URL is transmitted
  • Expo Push Notifications — delivers threat alerts to your device
  • Railway — hosts our backend server in the United States
5

App Permissions

  • Accessibility Service — reads on-screen text to detect scams across all apps. Can be disabled anytime in Android Settings
  • Receive SMS / Read SMS — monitors incoming texts for scam detection
  • Call Screening — analyzes incoming call numbers. Calls are never recorded
  • Contacts — reads contact list to avoid flagging messages from people you know
  • Camera — used only when you choose to scan a physical document
  • Foreground Service — keeps protection running in background. A notification is always shown
  • Internet — required to communicate with our threat analysis server
⚠️ All permissions are optional. You can revoke any permission at any time in Android Settings → Apps → Alertify.AI → Permissions.
6

Data Retention

Alert history stays on your device until you delete it. Uninstalling the app removes all locally stored data. Our servers retain no user-specific data after analysis is complete.

7

Security

  • HTTPS encryption for all data in transit
  • Local SQLite database with no remote access
  • No persistent storage of analyzed content on our servers
  • Regular security reviews of backend infrastructure
8

Children's Privacy

Alertify.AI is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect information from children. Contact us if you believe a child has provided personal information and we will promptly delete it.

9

Policy Changes

We may update this policy from time to time. Significant changes will be communicated by updating the date above and, where appropriate, through an in-app notification. Continued use after changes means you accept the updated policy.
